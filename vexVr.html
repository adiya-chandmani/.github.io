My VEX VR Projects

In my VEX VR Robotics project, I programmed a virtual robot to complete maze and color-sensing challenges. One of my main achievements was successfully coding the robot to collect colored disks and return them to the correct zones. I learned how to use control structures like loops and conditionals to make my robot behave smartly.
