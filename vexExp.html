My VEX EXP Projects

For the VEX EXP Robotic project, I built and programmed a physical robot that could pick up and move objects. I used VEXcode EXP to write programs that made the robot drive forward, turn precisely, and operate the arm to grab and release balls. I learned how to adjust motor speeds and use sensors to make the robot stop at certain distances. It was a great experience applying both hardware and software together.
